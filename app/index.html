Etsy Lights

I like Etsy. I love interior design. I'm a sucker for lighting.

So I made a site that I could stare at for hours. This pulls the most recent items posted to Etsy from the 'Industrial Chandelier Lighting' category*. You'll see the items changer everytime you refresh the page.

*'Categories' on Etsy are pretty flexible for their sellers. Sometimes you see things you just aren't looking for.
